The purpose of a paper presentation is to enhance a particular student’s ability in the art of academic writing or making papers in English and to present it. RULES
  • Maximum number of participants in a Team is 3.
  • Paper should be in IEEE Format.

  • Presentation document must be between 8- 15 pages.

  • Kindly bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen-drive or laptop can also be used to present your document
